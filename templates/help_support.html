Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can book directly through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our reservations team at +254 700 123 456.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before check-in receive a full refund. Late cancellations incur a one-night charge.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before check-in through your account or by contacting us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect all payment transactions.

When will I be charged?

For most reservations, we charge a 20% deposit at booking and the balance at check-in.

What time is check-in/check-out?

Check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in/late check-out may be available upon request.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we offer secure underground parking for all guests at no additional charge.

Is WiFi available?

Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the property.
