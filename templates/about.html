Our Story

Founded by aviation enthusiasts, Nairobi Jet House began with a single aircraft and a vision to make private air travel more accessible in East Africa. Today, we operate a growing fleet serving corporate clients, celebrities, and discerning travelers across the continent.

What sets us apart is our commitment to African hospitality combined with global aviation standards. Our team understands the unique travel needs within the region while maintaining international best practices.
